Peer Support Groups

Connect with others who understand your journey. Share, listen, and grow together in a supportive community.

Why Join Peer Support Groups?

  • Find a safe space to share your thoughts and feelings.
  • Learn coping strategies from those with similar experiences.
  • Reduce feelings of isolation and build a sense of belonging.
  • Receive encouragement and support from peers.

Recommended Platforms

7 Cups

7 Cups is an online platform offering peer-to-peer support and free counseling. Trained listeners provide a compassionate ear to help you manage feelings of depression and anxiety.

  • Features:
  • Free emotional support chats.
  • Guided mental health exercises.
  • Online communities for various topics.
Visit 7 Cups

Supportiv

Supportiv connects you with small peer groups for anonymous support. It focuses on real-time conversations tailored to your emotional needs.

  • Topic-based group discussions.
  • Confidential and judgment-free environment.
  • Resource suggestions for mental health challenges.
Visit Supportiv

Mental Health America

MHA offers online forums where you can connect with others experiencing similar challenges, share insights, and receive encouragement.

  • Peer-led support groups.
  • Resources for managing mental health.
  • Opportunities to share and listen.
Visit MHA
